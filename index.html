About me

My name is Annabelle. I'm 25 and I'm from Paris. I am currently working in asset management and I have an interest in technological innovation and startups.

In the past year, I have participated to an online technology entrepreneurship class offered by Stanford University and to a digital marketing class at General Assembly. During these courses I had the chance to work on an online social travel platform and to do a consultancy project for artspotter.com, a startup based in London focusing on social art discovery.

This summer, I am taking part to the Code First:Girls program run by Entrepreneur First as I want to learn how to code to be able to build prototypes and to work with technical teams.
